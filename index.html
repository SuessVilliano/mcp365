What is MCP365?

MCP365 is not just a server. It’s an intelligent control platform that unifies data ingestion, validation, AI reasoning, API execution, and real-time orchestration — all simultaneously, in milliseconds. Imagine a system where every action validates itself, cross-references history, checks logs, contacts clients, triggers trades, updates CRMs, and notifies agents at the same time.

Webhooks are linear. MCP365 is parallel, coordinated, AI-driven automation. From traders to business owners, MCP365 powers intelligent workflows that think, act, and respond instantly.
